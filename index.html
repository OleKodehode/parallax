One Piece

One Piece is a ongoing shounen manga and anime! It is adventure, friendship and traveling the sea as a pirate! The story itself focuses on a young man named Monkey D. Luffy that have a dream about becoming the king of all pirates! His inspiration for becoming a pirate came from his childhood where he met a powerfull pirate. Luffys journey is filled with alot of diffrent emotions, and he will meet many unique persons on his journey.

The Crew

The Crew

During the series we will meet alot of diffrent characters that will join in on Luffy's crews. Amoung these characters are Roronoa Zoro, the man that wants to become the world's strongest Swordsman. They will also meet a navigator that wants to draw a worldmap, a chef that believes in a old tale about a legendary sea, a man that lies just as much as Pinnochio, a reindeer that's also a doctor and a archelogosit. They will all be apart of The Straw Hat crew with Luffy as theire captain.

Going Merry

This is the Straw Hat Crews ship! She have been taking them all the way from East Sea and to the Grand Line. This ship is old but loved by all and have been trough so many more journeys than you would imagine. It's also said that this amazing pirateship can fly!
